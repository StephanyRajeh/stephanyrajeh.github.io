About me

Hello! I am an Assistant Professor at EFREI Paris-Panthéon-Assas Université and an associate member at the Complex Networks team in LIP6, Sorbonne Université. Previously, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher in the Complex Networks team in LIP6. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Burgundy, France.

During my Ph.D., my research involved studying the interplay between the network's community structure, the underlying dynamics, and identifying influential nodes in complex networks. This work was conducted under the supervision of Pr. Hocine Cherifi. As a postdoctoral researcher, I focused on anomaly detection in temporal networks with Matthieu Latapy.

My research interests include: complex networks, machine learning, economic and social sciences, and data science for social good.
